Global Arms Transfers (1950-2023)

by Calvin Lu


Network Data of Military Arms Transfers Around the World.


The project will illustrate the sale of military arms across the world. Military arms sales reflects fluctuating regional tensions and serves as an indication of international relationships. Since 1950, the world has witnessed arms races and military conflicts between countries and continents. By illustrating aggregated military arms transfers, the visualizations will display contrasting relationships between countries and regions. This project will generate a deeper curiosity of the sale of military arms and spark new inquiries for the causation of varying sales and purchases.


I hypothesize that the Western countries will be the most active in selling military arms.


We will be analyzing military arms transactions in aggregate between 1950 and 2023. The data driving this graph is sourced from the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute SIPRI Arms Transfer database annual military arms transfers. The graph to the right displays the aggregate of the top 100 military arms transactions between 1950-2023.
